Mayak Eggs

Mayak eggs are a popular Korean street food. They are eggs that are boiled, peeled, and then marinated in a sweet and spicy sauce. The eggs are then skewered and served on a stick. They are a delicious and satisfying snack that is perfect for any time of day.   Basically drugs in egg form. 10/10 would recommend :D

  • 6 eggs
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  1. Place the eggs in a saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a boil over high heat.
  2. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Drain the eggs and run under cold water to cool.
